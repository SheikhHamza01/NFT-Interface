Charity Initiatives

The story behind the PHC brand.

The project initiative is simple, inject a higher level of social responsibility into the world of NFTs & crytpocurrency. The homelessness epidemic in our backyard of Los Angeles has become a presence that one cannot simply turn a blind eye to any longer. Our team of like-minded individuals have banded together to create a digital solution to abolish this evergrowing social issue.

DEEP cuts in music & merchandise.

PHC has developed a true IRL backed utility. The ecosystem will be internally funded in the form of sales royalties from national merchandise retail partners. The PHC ambassador list will also continue to grow & evolve as we partner with top tier individuals from musicians to artists to photographers and tastemakers alike. PHC Fest is our pipe dream event that will take all of our community to achieve.

A wave of new existence PLAGUES our community.

The PHC’s evolution that will change genesis hands to an ungodly new existence. A digital plague will bring transformative attributes to our ecosystem, giving birth to new collective sets in the form of the device for transformation & the resulting entity, whatever that may be. Beyond the Plague comes the future of the PHC’s digital presence within the Metaverse. We will be exploring partnerships as we expand this project area.

A community governed giveback & charity DAO.

Giving the power to the people. We are supporting those who supported us. We give back generously to those in need & as an engaged family of HAND HODLERS, we have the power to change lives. PHC’s one-of-a-kind community governed DAO (Decentralized Automous Organization) will be used to vote on charity initiatives, distribute passive income in form of the utility token BLE$$ to our holders & act as the hub for the entire Praying Hands Club ecosystem.

A future of firsts including METACLOUT.

Metamerch is the future of digital fashion, and with our roots deep in merchandise, we are developing the next wave of wearables for the future of the metaverse. Building not only Metaclout for it’s owner, but these assets will be tradeable, swapable and sellable as a standard NFT. We are working with our retail partners to develop a Buy-One-Get-One style system to claim your Metamerch upon purchase.
